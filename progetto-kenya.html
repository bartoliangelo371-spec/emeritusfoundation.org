Brasov County, Romania To Kwale County, Kenya

"SPRING OF HEALING" ACADEMY

2019 – 2026

The story of this school was all about "NO"

NO Food NO Water NO Lavatories NO Clothes NO Shoes NO Chairs NO Desks NO Books NO HOPE.

Could we pretend it was not happening?
NO! We couldn't!
For us, this was the only acceptable negation in this story, so we did what was necessary so that all the "NO"s would turn into YES, because we knew that it is up to each and one of us, and together, we could be a family for these children and become their support and strength for a better future.

Our Core Goals

Our project is structured on 5 phases that extended on a 7-year period.

school

Education & Training

Providing quality basic education, training teachers, and supplying proper furniture and school materials.

health_and_safety

Health & Hygiene

Educating children about proper hygiene, providing health check-ups, separate restrooms, and immunization.

diversity_3

Community

Engaging the community in school management and creating safe, protective environments for learning and playing.

eco

Sustainability

Creating the conditions necessary for the school complex to self-sustain, including water supply and animal farming.

Construction of the first class. Providing complete furniture, school uniforms, health services, and a 1-year feeding program with permanent presence on site.

Covid-19 Era

During the covid-19 pandemic, we provided distance support and kept permanently in touch with our friends from the site. The number of supported children doubled.

face51 Children

Massive Expansion

Extension of the old structure into a school with 4 classrooms. Provided complete furniture, blackboards, science learning supplies, refurbished playground, and conducted an authorized hydro geological study.

water_drop
Feb 2023 - Mar 2024
face82 Children
water

Clean Water Arrives

From the very first moment it was mandatory to provide clean water. Nowadays, all the village of Mrima has drinkable clean water! Water means life, healthy conditions, working the land, breeding animals, and absolute progress for the entire community.

eco
Dec 2024 - Mar 2026

Self-Sustainability

LIONS plans to create conditions for the school complex to self-sustain without outside help, creating agricultural production and a small animal farm to support the nutrition of the children.

Final Master Project

Irrigation Project for Capsicum Cultivation

Lions Emeritus Brașov will support an irrigation system helping the community become completely self-sufficient through agriculture.
